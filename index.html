Hi! I'm Becky Morganna Trevelyan!

I am a 33 year old Transgender woman from Seattle, WA in the USA. I enjoy cooking and reading about the limitations of our species and their reactions when pushed to the absolute peak of their patience. I also greatly enjoy memes and writing about nonsense and the human mind, along with finding new things to learn and new art to consume I do actually create and share my own.

As a student I am aimed more towards AI but I have a lot of focuses that keep me busy during COVID times. Future Tech Astronomy Robotics Artificial Intelligence Photography Music Video Games Art

I am a Software engineering student at Thinkful working towards a career in AI and with space technologies. Growing up with two family businesses revolving around a lot of algebra, I was inspired to pursue STEM fields and the arts through my entire life and have become an aspiring tattoo artist with half a dozen clients.
